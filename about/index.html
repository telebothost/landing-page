About TeleBotHost

TeleBotHost is created to make Telegram bot hosting simple, fast, and accessible to everyone — developers, creators, and businesses.

Our Mission

We aim to provide the smoothest and most reliable platform for running Telegram bots without requiring servers, complex setups, or technical barriers. TeleBotHost focuses on performance, stability, and developer comfort.

What We Provide

From automatic hosting to a clean dashboard, sandbox protection, security layers, TBL language, and official libraries — everything is built to help you deploy bots instantly with zero hassle.

Core Values

Reliability

Always-online uptime with fast execution, stable servers, and multi-region redundancy.

Simplicity

Designed for both beginners and experts — deploy and manage your bots with ease.

Security First

All tokens encrypted, sandbox running, DDoS protected, and no user data logging.

Performance

Optimized engine, TBL language enhancements, and 200ms anti-flooding delay for smooth bot behavior.

Our Vision

We want TeleBotHost to become the #1 platform for Telegram automation — where developers build, scale, and innovate without limits.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I deploy a Telegram bot on TBH?

    A: Sign up and upload your own bot files or choose a template from the community store. No coding or server setup is needed.

  • Q: Does TBH provide free hosting?

    A: Yes, we offer a **free plan** to get started. Paid plans are available for higher uptime and more resources.

  • Q: Can I connect my bot to a custom domain?

    A: At the moment, **custom domains are not supported**. You’ll get a secure TBH subdomain for your bot’s API endpoint.

  • Q: Can I upload my own code or just use templates?

    A: Both options are available. You can deploy **ready-made bots** from the store or upload your **custom scripts** via the file manager or GitHub integration.

  • Q: Does TBH provide database support?

    A: Yes, TBH includes a built-in database for user data, logs, and session management. External databases (like MongoDB or Firebase) can also be connected.

  • Q: Is my bot online 24/7?

    A: Absolutely. All bots hosted on TBH run on **cloud servers** and stay live 24/7 as long as your plan supports it.

  • Q: Where can I get help if I’m stuck?

    A: Join our Telegram Support Group or visit the Documentation for detailed guides and examples.

  • Q: Can I monetize my bot on TBH?

    A: Yes, you can integrate **payment gateways, ads, or premium features** within your bot. TBH does not take a commission.

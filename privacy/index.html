Welcome to Tele Bot Host ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our TBL bot hosting services.

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our services:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

We require all third parties to respect the security of your personal data and to treat it in accordance with the law.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:
